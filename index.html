Our Promise

Here is what we are promising the country and more. You can always hold us accountable to them.

Education

We aim at the expansion of education across all fields, voluntary and private organizations to establish skills acquisition centers through:

• Provision of free, functional, and compulsory primary to junior secondary education.
• Integration of skill acquisitions as part of the formal learning curriculum at secondary and tertiary levels;
• Empower secondary school leavers with entrepreneurial skill sets through sponsored workshops;
• Encourage IT skills among learners by providing IT centers; • Encourage private bodies to sponsor research programs for students in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) related fields.

Healthcare

We intend to make our health care system in Nigeria an enviable one by: • Providing quality and accessible healthcare to all citizens.
• Improve the health status of all Nigerians.
• Integrate the use of advanced medical technology in our health care sector;
• Advanced training for doctors and better remuneration for medical personnel;
• See an increase in the number of qualified doctors in the country;
• Advance in our ability to do medical research and produce drugs and vaccines.

Economy

The party aims at providing and creating room for industrialization in local, state, and federal constituencies by:

• Privatization of public enterprises that can be sustained by private entrepreneurs;
• Encouraging small scale businesses through simplified registration processes and loan assistance;
• Inclusion of at least 80% of Nigerian citizens in foreign industries in Nigeria to reduce unemployment;
• Sponsoring research on new technologies to enhance the industrial sector; • Encouraging industrial investments by lowering interest rates;

Agriculture

The bedrock of the Nigerian economy in time past was agriculture. After the discovery of crude oil, our agricultural sector gradually became neglected. We want to revitalize this sector because of its importance in food, clothing, and shelter by:

• Create room for technological advancement in our agricultural process;
• Help in the purchase of high-end equipment for farmers in each state;
• Looking into large scale farming;
• Training of farmers on modern and best practices in farming;
• Rewarding active and innovative farmers

Power

The Young Coalition Party strongly desires to improve and enhance energy and electricity supply in all parts of the country through:

• Create efficient energy generation and distribution companies;
• Maintain, upgrade and equip the energy generation and distribution facilities;
• Encourage and support energy and electricity research and development centers in Nigeria;
• Promote the use of solar energy;
• Expand sources of energy;
• Ensure the use of renewable energy.

Water Supply

Water is an essential part of human life. It is inhuman not to make it available for consumption and general use.

• Encourage local production of standard water treatment chemicals;
• Construction of treatment and water recycling plant;
• Facilitate water transportation;
• Create educational awareness;
• Recycling wastewater;
• Improve irrigation;
• Provide energy efficient diesel plants;
• Protection of water against pollution and contamination in urban, semi-urban, and rural areas.

Security

Attainment of a peaceful Nigerian society is YCP's top priority, even as national security issues are big challenges in Nigeria's effort to develop. But the YCP in government shall create and maintain a society where there is no threat of life and property. We aim at ending terrorism armed robbery,kidnapping.

• Make provision for the training of security on how to utilize modern technology in curbing security challenges;
• Ensure adequate funding of the police and other security agencies to motivate and the right attitude to work;
• Ensuring IT literacy is a criterion for recruitment into the police and other security agencies.

Housing

Youth coalition party promises to ensure a safe, secure, and conducive environment for all citizens of the country by;

• Providing loans at low interest to landowners for an extended period to enable quick completion of homes;
• Giving land to citizens based on mapped out plans for housing and urban development for the state;
• Formulating resettlement policies that will be implemented effectively on the occasion of natural disaster, government projects, or unforeseen losses; • Ensuring landowners construct a standard drainage system to reduce flooding and loss of properties;
• Encouraging private owned real estate companies to build houses to sell to the public at affordable prices;

Tourism

To ensure our country, Nigeria, remains welcoming for tourists and a haven during visits.

• Reorganize the board in charge of tourism in the state to function independently;
• Formulate new and modern policies that promote tourism;
• Encouraging private entrepreneurs, Non-Governmental Organizations to invest in the development of the tourist sector;
• Monitoring the activities of the National Tourism Board, private bodies, and state government to ensure harmony in decision making.

Employment

Nigeria has been tackled and ambushed by unemployment affecting the image of the nation. The party sees the need for members of society to have access to jobs.

• Tackle the issue of corruption in the civil and public service and also in every office of the federation;
• Create Industrial friendly environment;
• Reform the educational system;
• Provide incentives for entrepreneurs and local companies;
• Freedom of labor movement;
• Make job creation friendly policies.
